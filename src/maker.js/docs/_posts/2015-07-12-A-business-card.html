--- layout: post title: A laser cut business card ---

I recently attended the 2015 CascadiaFest conference. It was an inspiring event with great talks and great folks. I wanted generate some interest around Maker.js, so I thought I'd "print" up a few business cards. If you were at the conference, maybe you got one of these:

In case you can't tell, I themed this business card like a toy model car kit - the kind where you break away the plastic pieces. I used the current model code for the logo and I needed to create a whole new stencil font to add the URL you see in the picture. The font is incomplete, and only has the letters for "makerjs.org" but its a good start, and ripe for contributors to jump in ;-)

Head over to the demos page and look for "card" in the dropdown and customize your own version.
